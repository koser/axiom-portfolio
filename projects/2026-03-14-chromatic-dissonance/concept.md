# Chromatic Dissonance
**Date:** 2026-03-14
**Library:** canvas

## Concept
A field of overlapping circular forms that slowly drift and phase through each other, creating constantly shifting interference patterns of color. The composition maintains perpetual near-collision—shapes approach alignment but never quite resolve, sustaining visual tension between harmony and discord. Time dilates: movement occurs at the threshold of perception, rewarding sustained attention.

## Palette
Primary process colors (cyan, magenta, yellow) plus deep violet, deployed with additive blending modes to create optical mixing zones. Background remains stark white to maximize chromatic intensity and figura-ground ambiguity.

## Interaction
Mouse position subtly warps the gravitational field governing orbital drift—an invisible influence rather than direct manipulation. Clicks introduce momentary turbulence that decays over 30-40 seconds. The system resists control, making interaction feel more like atmospheric perturbation than command.
